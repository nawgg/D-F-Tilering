About us

Welcome to D&F – Tailoring Experts.
With over 22 years of experience in the tailoring industry, D&F is proud to offer expert craftsmanship and personalized service. For the past four years, we've been serving the Vancouver Island community, bringing global expertise and attention to detail to every piece we work on.

From bridal alterations to modern fashion tailoring and everyday repairs, we’re passionate about helping you look and feel your best. Our skilled team treats each garment with precision and care, ensuring a perfect fit tailored to your unique style.

Whether you need a quick zipper replacement, dry cleaning, or a complete wardrobe update, we’re here to help. Our mission is to make sure you feel confident and comfortable—no matter the occasion.

Have a question? We’re always happy to help you. Let D&F help you achieve the perfect fit.

Thank you for choosing D&F Tailoring Experts. We look forward to working with you!
